Yummy and Easy Breakfast Crépes

Crépes

Directions:

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 0.5 cup of milk
  • 0.5 cup of water
  • 0.25 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter

Directions

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, water, and salt together in a large mixing bowl
  2. Add flour and butter, whisk until smooth
  3. Cover mixing bowl and let sit overnight, or for 4 hours in the refridgerator
  4. Remove mixing bowl and heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour or scoop batter, using roughly 0.75 cups for each crépe. If needed, tilt the pan to make it more circular.
  6. Cook like pancake, looking at the edges for bubbles, until the other side is light brown. Roughly 1 to 2 minutes per side. Set crépes aside until finished.
  7. Serve with any toppings in any fold prefered. Included is a recipe for a lemon curd filling that goes well. Often, powdered sugar is a nice topping for it as well.

Lemon Curds

Directions:

Ingredients

  • 0.75 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 0.75 cup of white sugar
  • 0.5 cup of unsalted butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of grated lemon zest

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan.
  2. Cook over medium-low heat, constantly whisk to prevent eggs from curdling, until bubbles begin to rise to the surface. Roughly 5 or 6 minutes.
  3. Transfer sauce into a bowl, and keep in refridgerator, most likely alongside crépes. It will thicken as it cools.
  4. Place inside crépe and enjoy!
